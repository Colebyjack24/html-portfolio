My Journey

Originally from Sacramento, CA, I grew up with an inclination for creativity. I explore sounds and ideas that look beautiful to my eye. I developed these skills while going through High School where I was following the path of the Engineering Academy, normally, this would lead students to enroll in a University Engineering Undergraduate program, however under pressure frome exestential fear of life becoming "boring" I chose to look for another career path. While my desire remained to drop out and make music, I never really wanted to dissapoint how much money my parents put into my education.

So while studying to get a degree in Community Health Sciences I maintained a constant pulse with local music scenes in Reno, NV where I went to school. I became a successful music producer for other artists on the side. I found my passion regardless even if it wasn't paying all the bills. I graduated right before COVID and fell into an open market for employment so I could continue to explore career paths which again I switched to something I felt would give me leverage into a career involved with creativity.

Here I am before you in an IT career writing an about me section on a HTML website. In this journey I found Jesus, friends, highs, lows, a beautiful smart wife, an amazing child, and a comfort beyond compare getting to make what I love and do things that are interesting.
